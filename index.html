Encounter Overview

The Pope is a three-phase cathedral-style fantasy encounter with one major add intermission. The raid succeeds by handling decree positioning, locking down Cardinal casts, and keeping Swiss Guards controlled in the add lane.

Raid size: 20Heroism: 30%Main challenge: decrees + add control

Trash: Cardinal Conclave

Red Hat Edict marks a player. Nearby players take heavy Holy damage after the timer.
Excommunication is an interruptible silence. Assign kicks.
Smoke of Deliberation gives the Cardinal a haste burst. Stun, purge, or kite during the buff.

Trash: Swiss Guard Honorbound

Halberd Sweep is a frontal cone. Tanks face guards away.
Ceremonial Formation reduces damage taken while guards are grouped. Drag them apart.
You Shall Not Queue-Jump charges the furthest player.

Phase 1: Audience

  • Kneel: stop moving.
  • Confess: stack in assigned pairs.
  • Repent: spread at least 8 yards.
Incense Clouds apply stacking Lightheaded Piety.
Blessing or Rebuke should be dispelled after the safe timing, not instantly.

Intermission: The Conclave Votes

  1. Cardinal of Silence — control first.
  2. Cardinal of Doctrine — interrupt Unquestionable Truth.
  3. Cardinal of Smoke — limits visibility.
  4. Cardinal of Coin — avoid gold piles.

Phase 2: Urbi et Orbi

Balcony Benediction sweeps across the arena. Avoid completely.
Keys of the Kingdom rotate and knock players back.
Canon Law drops permanent seal zones. Place them at the edge.
Indulgence creates a shield. Swap to Donation Chests.

Phase 3: Infallibility Protocol

Infallible Pronouncement is a raid-wide blast. Use confession booths.
Confession Booths hold only three players.
Holy See, Holy Do repeats the last two decrees.
Final Amen ramps raid damage until the encounter ends.

Role Notes

  • Tanks: swap at 2 stacks. Off-tank controls Swiss Guards.
  • Healers: hold cooldowns for decree failures and Final Amen.
  • DPS: interrupts first, add control second, boss uptime third.

Raid Leader Callouts

  • “Kneel — stop moving.”
  • “Confess — pair stacks.”
  • “Repent — spread.”
  • “Cardinals active — kicks assigned.”
  • “Swiss Guards — off tank pick up.”
  • “Drop Canon Law at edge.”

Mythic-Only: Habemus Papam

Whenever a Cardinal is resolved, nominated players emit White Smoke. Nominated players move to assigned markers and stay separated.
