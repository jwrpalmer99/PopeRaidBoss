Pull Plan

🛡Main tank faces The Pope away from raid.
✦Interrupt Cardinals immediately.
⛑Off-tank Swiss Guards away from ranged.
🏹Ranged stay spread unless Confess is called.
✚Healers hold CDs for decrees and Final Amen.

Encounter Overview

The Pope is a three-phase cathedral encounter with one major add intermission. The raid wins by cleanly handling decree positioning, keeping Cardinal casts locked down, and preventing Swiss Guards from turning the backline into a confessional booth queue.

Raid size: 20Damage: Holy / Fire / BureaucraticHeroism: 30%Wipe cause: failed decrees + loose adds

Trash: Cardinal Conclave

Red Hat Edict marks a player. After 5 seconds, nearby players take heavy Holy damage.
Excommunication is an interruptible silence. Assign kicks; do not let it complete.
Smoke of Deliberation gives the Cardinal a vanish/reappear haste burst. Stun, purge, or kite during the buff.

Tactic: spread loosely, rotate interrupts, and focus one Cardinal at a time. If three Cardinals free-cast together, expect healer panic and Discord silence.

Trash: Swiss Guard Honorbound

Halberd Sweep is a frontal cone. Tanks face guards away from the group.
Ceremonial Formation links nearby guards and reduces damage taken. Drag them apart.
You Shall Not Queue-Jump charges the furthest player. Ranged should not stand at the absolute edge unless baiting intentionally.

Tactic: off-tank gathers them, then pulls them out of formation. Melee stay behind, ranged avoid baiting multiple charges.

Trash: Choir of Endless Amen

Sustained Note causes raid-wide ticking damage.
Off-Key Hymn fears a random player and can send them through bad zones.
Perfect Harmony is lethal if three choir members complete a cast together.

Tactic: assign interrupts before the pull. Use AoE stuns when casts overlap. Kill one choir member quickly to break the harmony check.

Phase 1: Audience with The Pope

Papal Decree announces one of three commands: Kneel, Confess, or Repent.
  • Kneel: stop moving for 3 seconds.
  • Confess: stack in assigned pairs.
  • Repent: spread at least 8 yards.
Incense Clouds drift around the room and apply stacking Lightheaded Piety. Avoid unless you enjoy healers saying your name like a swear word.
Blessing or Rebuke must be dispelled after 4 seconds, not instantly. Early dispel splashes the raid.

Positioning: main tank in front, melee behind The Pope, ranged in a back arc, healers rear-centre.

Intermission: The Conclave Votes

At 70%, The Pope becomes immune and four Cardinals enter. The room fills with vote smoke and mechanic clutter.

  1. Cardinal of Silence — stun/kill first or the raid loses interrupts.
  2. Cardinal of Doctrine — interrupt Unquestionable Truth.
  3. Cardinal of Smoke — limits visibility and makes decree calls harder.
  4. Cardinal of Coin — drops gold piles that explode when touched.

Ballots of Faith: defeated Cardinals drop ballots. Collect enough to end the intermission and force The Pope back into the fight.

Phase 2: Urbi et Orbi

Balcony Benediction sweeps a huge frontal beam across the room. Touching it is fatal or close enough to fatal that the difference is academic.
Keys of the Kingdom rotate around the arena and knock players back. Watch your feet and avoid being punted into decree zones.
Canon Law marks three players. After 8 seconds, each drops a permanent legal seal zone. Drop these at the edge.
Indulgence shields The Pope. DPS swap to Donation Chests to remove the shield.

Positioning: drag the boss away from permanent zones. Ranged remain loose, but leave paths open for beam dodges.

Phase 3: Infallibility Protocol

At 30%, The Pope descends and the encounter becomes a burn phase.

Infallible Pronouncement is a raid-wide blast. Damage is reduced by standing inside confession booths.
Confession Booths hold only three players. Overcrowding causes Awkward Silence, stunning everyone inside.
Holy See, Holy Do repeats the last two Papal Decrees back-to-back.
Final Amen summons choir spirits that ramp raid damage until The Pope dies.

Plan: assign booth groups before pull, use Heroism/Bloodlust at 30%, and chain healing cooldowns during Final Amen.

Tank Assignments

  • Main Tank: hold The Pope facing north/upstage and away from the raid.
  • Swap: taunt at 2 stacks of Burden of Office, or sooner if a decree overlap is coming.
  • Off Tank: collect Swiss Guards quickly, pull them out of Ceremonial Formation, and keep their frontals off ranged.
  • Phase 2: move The Pope away from Canon Law zones and leave safe lanes for beam dodges.

Healer Assignments

  • Do not instantly dispel Blessing or Rebuke; wait for the safe timing.
  • Use spot healing on players with high Lightheaded Piety stacks.
  • Prepare raid cooldowns for failed decrees, Balcony Benediction overlaps, and Final Amen.
  • In Phase 3, assign healers to booth groups so no booth is abandoned.

DPS Assignments

  • Priority 1: interrupt and kill Cardinals.
  • Priority 2: help the off-tank stabilise Swiss Guards if they start cleaving the raid.
  • Priority 3: destroy Donation Chests during Indulgence.
  • Save burst for intermission adds and the 30% burn.

Raid Leader Callouts

  • “Kneel — stop moving.”
  • “Confess — pair stacks now.”
  • “Repent — spread.”
  • “Cardinals active — kicks assigned.”
  • “Swiss Guards loose — off tank pick up.”
  • “Drop Canon Law at edge.”
  • “Booths in 3 — assigned groups only.”

Mythic-Only: Habemus Papam

Whenever a Cardinal dies, one or more players are secretly nominated. After 15 seconds, nominated players emit White Smoke. If two nominated players stand within 10 yards, the raid wipes to There Can Be Only One Pope.

Handling: nominated players call their names, move to pre-assigned smoke markers, and do not chase parses across the room.

Achievements

  • No Pope, No Hope: defeat The Pope without any player failing a Papal Decree.
  • Smoke Break: defeat the Conclave while every Cardinal of Smoke is active.
  • Bless Me, Raid Leader: defeat The Pope after at least 10 players gain 5 stacks of Lightheaded Piety.
