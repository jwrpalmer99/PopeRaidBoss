Positioning Summary

Main Tank: in frontMelee: behind The PopeOff Tank: Swiss GuardsRanged: spread arcHealers: rearCardinals: interrupt / focus

Phase 1: Audience

  • Main Tank holds The Pope facing away from the raid.
  • Melee stack behind the boss and avoid the frontal cone.
  • Ranged spread in a loose arc for decree mechanics.
  • Healers stay at the rear with clear lines of sight.

Add Priority

  1. Cardinals — interrupt casts and focus them down.
  2. Swiss Guards — off tank picks them up and keeps them away from the raid.
  3. Mechanic objects — swap when The Pope shields.

Key Mechanics

Papal Decree

  • Kneel: stop moving.
  • Confess: stack in pairs.
  • Repent: spread out.

Incense Clouds

Avoid drifting smoke. Too many stacks cause unnecessary healer pain and raid-leader disappointment.

Role Notes

  • Tanks: swap on stacks. Off tank catches Swiss Guards immediately.
  • Healers: hold cooldowns for decree failures and late-phase raid damage.
  • DPS: stop tunnelling. Cardinals are not decorative.

Mythic Reminder

Habemus Papam: nominated players must spread. If two nominated players overlap, the raid wipes to There Can Be Only One Pope.
